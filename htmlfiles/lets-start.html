What is space ?

Space is nothing but emptiness that contain whatever you see hear or feel. "Space is almost perfect vaccum i.e. nearly void of matter and with extremely low pressure."

NOTE:Vaccum or Void means "emptiness or nothingness".

Why is space dark ?

In space there is no atmosphere for scattering of light to see colours i.e. absence of all light(colours) eventually leads to darkness.

NOTE: Atmosphere is dense layers of gases that surround us.

What are planets ?

Planets are large almost round bodies in space that moves around a star. Eg.Earth

What are stars?

Stars is gaint ball of gases that burns itself to provide the heat and light that help sustain life on earth. Eg.Sun

What is Solar System?

The Solar System consists of the Sun and eight others planets along with their satellites and other celestial bodies that revolve around it.

What are Satellites?

A Satellite is object in space that revolve around planets. Eg.Moon

NOTE: a)Rotation: Moving of object around its own axis. b)Revolution: Moving of object around another object.

What are the name of eight planets in our solar system?

  • Mercury: Closest to sun
  • Venus: Hottest planet
  • Earth: Blue planet
  • Mars: Red planet
  • Jupiter: Biggest planet
  • Saturn: Planet having ring of celestial bodies around it
  • Uranus: Green planet
  • Neptune: Farthest planet

What are celestial bodies?

All the objects that is outside the Earth's atmosphere is called celestial bodies. Eg.Sun ,Moon ,other planets and their satellites ,asteroids ,meteoroids ,comets.

What are asteriods ,meteroides and comets ?

Asteriods: Small rocky objects that revolve around the sun along with other planets and their satellite. Meteoroids: A small body in solar system that becomes meteoroid when it enters Earth's atmosphere. Comets:A object in space that looks like bright star with a tail and moves around the Sun.They are also known as "Shooting Stars".

How far away is Sun and Moon from Earth ?

Sun is approximately 148.64 millions km far away from Earth Moon is approximately 384,400 km far away from Earth

How long does it take light to reach Earth from Sun and Moon?

From Sun it takes 8 minutes 20 seconds From Moon it takes 1.3 seconds

Can Humans go out of Earth?

Yes.The people who do so are called Astronauts.They go in Space with the help of huge Rockets and then use the same rocket to come back to Earth.

Light must be fast to reach that far away so quickly.What is the speed of Light?

Speed of light is approximately 300,000 km/second

Why does Sky appears blue at daytime and black at night?

The Sky is Blue due to scattering of sunlight and Dark at night due to absence of Sun.

NOTE:"Scattering" is spreading of sunlight in all direction because of dust particles present in atmosphere.
